Our Mission

Under the steadfast leadership of its Chairman, His Holiness Naeb Quthbuzzaman Dr. Sheikh Nizamudhin Sulthan Shah Qadiri Chishti, Jeelani Trust carries forward the foundational objectives set by the revered Sufi Guru, His Holiness Quthbuzzaman Dr. Sheikh Yousuf Sulthan Shah Qadiri Chishti. Continuing the profound legacy of its founder, the current leadership firmly advances the noble mission of Jeelani Trust, expanding its vital work of promoting Sufi perfection of human life, sponsoring developments in education and health, helping the poor and needy, and spreading peace and harmony in society. Based at Jeelani Shareef in Kerala, the Trust's core commitment remains to serve society and nurturing a morally robust humanity.

Upholding our wider vision and addressing the pressing needs of the time, Jeelani Trust keeps devising effective plans and projects, and systematically carries them out on regular basis. The Trust conducts programmes and runs institutions for promoting holistic well-being, expanding educational development, healthcare initiatives, and humanitarian aid, grounded in the principles of Sufi piety and socio-religious reformation, for the benefit of the deserving and under-served communities within the region and around the world.

Jeelani Trust is keen on advancing its activities and initiatives in various fields, including (but not limited to):

  • Promoting human development through Sufi moral values and practices, propagating the traditional Qadiri – Chishti Sufi path among all sections of society, providing spiritual guidance, classes and conventions, enabling people for ethical life, and ensuring peace in life and hereafter.
  • Keeping access open for all to the global Sufi centre of peace and spiritual blessings, Quthbuzzaman Jeelani Shareef, providing guidance in Prophetic teachings for the ideal humanity.
  • Encouraging and sponsoring the integration of Islamic and liberal education, and in-depth research and practice in Sufism; Promoting cutting edge studies in science and technology, (especially in ICT), with ethical guidance and moral values – ‘Nizamia Institute’ being a comprehensive example.
  • Establish educational institutions and excellence centres – colleges and institutes for studies in STEM, Liberal Arts, and Religious Studies; run orphanages, high schools, educational & vocational training centers, institutes for ICT, etc. for the educational advancement, not only of the Muslim religious minority, but for the society at large.
  • Establish and maintain masjids, Sufi research centre, special centres for traditional Islamic & Arabic studies, offline and online Madrasas – from preschool to higher secondary – Jeelani masjid, Nizamia Institute, Nizamia Madrasa, etc. are among many functional models.
  • Develop Libraries & Research Institutes, special centres for advanced studies and career guidance, coaching institutes for competitive exams supportive especially of the weaker social section - Quthbuzzaman Global Sufi Research Centre, Gurukulam Nizamia, Nizamia Centre for Excellence, to name a few ventures in this connection.
  • Providing free education, off-line and online, at pre-school, primary, secondary, intermediate, undergraduate and postgraduate levels, enabling students for the future of advancements, and grounding them in moral vision – the multi-layered educational platforms of ‘Nizamia’ stand to fulfill this mission.
  • Ensuring wholistic empowerment of social and religious minorities and weaker sections through healthcare, education, and employment; providing philanthropic aid and setting up enabling measures.
  • Public championing of religious harmony, and campaigning against terrorism and communalism, conducting conferences and mass-rallies to promote peace and harmony.
  • Encouraging inter-faith dialogue and propagating pluralist unity in society.
  • Educating the youth in ethical lifestyle and moral leadership, with entrepreneurial success for social care; building awareness against drug and anti-social elements.
  • Conducting frequent humanitarian reach-outs and welfare initiatives.
  • Establishing Hospitals, conducting medical camps and awareness programmes, providing healthcare amenities, catering to the health and well-being of the society in general, and poor and minorities in particular – Thaqdees Hospital, run by the Chairman of the Trust, stands as an outstanding initiative serving the society in the field of health and care.
  • Aiding public schools and offices for renovations; and supporting developments in infrastructure and public utility services.
  • Massively endorsing environmental care and sustenance of nature, etc., by conducting awareness programmes and seminars, supporting heritage-conservatory and eco-friendly projects, and leading a plant-a-tree drive on the World Environment Day, etc.
