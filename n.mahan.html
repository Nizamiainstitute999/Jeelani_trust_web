Chairman

What we need to do is to regain the original purity of our hearts and become truly human. Only then can we die happily as humans with clarity. What I am showing you is the way up there. Quthbuzzaman has shown us the path to elevate those who approach with sincere intention, by nurturing the divine qualities within, and lead them to the lofty state of being God's most excellent creation. That is what I continue to do. It is the method of spiritual purification that will continue until the Day of Judgment. Quthbuzzaman taught us to look at the heart of a person, regardless of caste or creed. It is when one attains that blessed state of mind that one achieves complete success in life. That is what I guarantee.


— Nāeb Quthbuzzāman Dr. Sheikh Nizamudhin Sulthan Shāh Qādiri Chishti

Naeb Quthbuzzaman Dr. Sheikh Nizamudhin Sulthan Shah Qadiri Chishti is a Sufi master, socio-religious reformer, and a true champion of communal harmony, futuristic education, humanitarian healthcare, and philanthropic support. Being the current leading master of Qadiriyyah Sufi path of peace and love and the custodian of ‘Quthbuzzaman Jeelani Shareef - Kerala’, the global centre of peace and humanity, Dr. Sheikh Nizamudhin Sulthan Shah Qadiri Chishti is the successor of the renowned modern Indian Sufi master and scholar Hazrat / His Holiness Quthbuzzaman Dr. Shaikh Sufi Muhammad Yousuf Sulthan Shah Qadiri Chishti, the reviver of Sufism in modern Kerala and the founder of ‘Jeelani Trust’. With the evidently increased impetus and enormous growth and impact, Sheikh Nizamudhin Sulthan is taking forward Hazrat Quthbuzzaman’s multifaceted undertakings for rebuilding an ethical humanity and progressive unity among social and religious communities.

Under the functional platform of Jeelani Trust and beyond, Sheikh Nizamudhin Sulthan carries out various missions, to cater the educational, health, economic, and spiritual needs and aspirations of the people within the region and outside. Dr. Sheikh Nizamudhin Sulthan is meticulously leading a devout and massive mission to bring about inspirational hope and eventual happiness to the humanity at large, widely opening the Sufi path of peace and love, practically and philosophically based on Islamic traditions and humanist outlook. He is the frontrunner of the ‘Jeelani’ movement of social reform and Sufi guidance, ‘Ghausiyya’ council of Islamic scholars, ‘Nizamia’ ventures of Islamic and liberal education, and ‘Thaqdees’ enterprise for the healthcare and wellness. He has been also compassionate in leading several humanitarian charity missions across diverse social sectors, including education, health, public safety, etc. To the peace-seekers from various social and religious backgrounds who visit Quthbuzzaman Jeelani Shareef, Sheikh Nizamudhin Sulthan keenly attends with moral guidance and spiritual blessings.

As a socially committed Sufi leader with a universal orientation and imaginative projects, Dr. Sheikh Nizamudhin Sulthan's ongoing mission eloquently embodies two themes of paramount global salience: universal social harmony and world peace. His predominant task is that of a compassionate Sufi, driven by a global vision and expansive outreach, dedicated to realizing an ethically empowered and spiritually enlightened humanity amidst the precarities of international conflicts and environmental crises. His teachings and initiatives underscore a noble mission he continues to accomplish for all who reach out to him is soulful guidance for ethical self-becoming and excellence in life. His significant public efforts to promote peace and harmony were officially recognized with an Honorary Doctorate in 'Human Rights & Peace' in 2021, conferred by the UN-accredited Globe Eagle University, authorized by the government of California, USA.

In the sacred chain (‘silsila’) of Qadiri Sufi masters, following the founding father Hazrat Quthbuzzaman’s Sufi path of spiritual awakening in the tradition of Tawhid and the luminous light (‘Nur’) of the holy Prophetic (peace and blessings be upon him), Sufi Guru Hazrat Naeb Quthbuzzaman Dr. Shaikh Nizamuddin Sulthan Shah Qadiri Chishti stands as the 36th spiritual successor of Prophet Muhammad (pbuh), and was chosen as the 19th Sufi descendant of Ghaus al-A’zam Sheikh ‘Muhyiddin’ Abdul Qadir al-Jeelani (may he be blessed), the supreme master and the founder of Qadiri Sufi path, established in early medieval Baghdad – in present-day Iraq. Taking forward Hazrat Quthbuzzaman’s phenomenal mission of reviving the pristine human spirit of din (religion) and illuminating the divine Light of the Holy Prophet (pbuh) in the human hearts, Hazrat Naeb Quthbuzzaman Sheikh Nizamudhin Sulthan insists on reviving the more amicable life style of traditional Islam, the foundation of which is Sufi path, grounded in tawhid (divine unity), tasawwuf (spiritual purity), guided by sunnah (close adherence to the pious traditions of the Prophet - pbuh). Countering and correcting the divisive ideologies of politically motivated fundamentalists and modernist religious extremists, Sheikh Nizamudhin Sulthan Shah faithfully follows in the footsteps of Hazrat Quthbuzzaman’s cherished Sufi tradition and humanitarian teachings. He seeks to revive the essential Sufi vitality of Islamic tradition, acclaimed for its clarity in envisioning humanity and practical ability to spiritually reunify human societies, transcending socio-political divisions and cultural distances.

Sheikh Nizamudhin Sulthan was born on 5th May 1975, at Aluva (Ernakulam), Kerala.

  • Born on the blessed urs day of Ghaus-e-A’zam Shaikh Abdul Qadir al-Jeelani (r), 11th Rabi-ul-Akhir.
    • ✦ In the cosmopolitan city of Kochi, to a family with long history of Islamic learning and Sufi mission, emerged out of Indo-Arab maritime cultural crossroads.
    • ✦ Named after the renowned 14th century Sufi, Hazrat Shaikh Nizamuddin Auliya of Delhi.
  • Islamic and modern education at Aluva, hometown at Ernakulam district, Cochin-Kerala.
  • Spiritual training at the early age under his father and renowned Sufi Shaikh, Quthbuzzaman Dr. Shaikh Yousuf Sulthan Shah Qadiri Chishti.
  • Received Khilafat, as the Sufi successor & Shah Qadiri Chishti Khalifa of Quthbuzzaman, on 17 August 2009, in the Grand Sufi Conference held at Jeelani Shareef – Cochin, Kerala.
  • Honoured (along with Hazrat Quthbuzzaman) at Ajmer Shareef, well-known Chishti Sufi centre in south Asia, at the dargah of Hazrat Khwaja Mo’inuddin Chishti, in 2016.
  • Hazrat Quthbuzzaman proclaimed his major Khalifa and the eldest son Sheikh Nizamudhin Sulthan Shah Qadiri Chishti to be the successor ('Nāeb') in all his spiritual authority,
    • ✦ At Baghdad Shareef, the Qadiriyya Mausoleum of the Grand Sufi Master Sheikh Abdul Qadir al-Jeelani (may he be blessed), during the historic function of honour to Hazrat Quthbuzzaman by the Qadiriyya Sufi authority at Baghdad.
    • ✦ Quthbuzzaman, Nāeb Quthbuzzaman and a huge delegation of 786 disciples were hosted by the Religious Department / Sunni Awqaf of the Government of Iraq, in December 2017.
      • ➤ In the immediate aftermath of the wisaal of Hazrat Quthbuzzaman (on 25 January 2019), Naeb Quthbuzzaman Dr. Shaikh Nizamuddin Sulthan takes charge of the leadership of the entire ‘Jeelani’ Sufi initiatives under Jeelani Shareef, being already nominated as the Chairman of Jeelani Charitable Trust, as well as Thaqdees Hospitals Ltd.

Committed to the multipronged Jeelani-Sufi mission, Naeb Quthbuzzaman Dr. Sheikh Nizamudhin Sulthan Shah Qadiri Chishti has dedicated his life to rekindle the sublime message of harmony among individuals, places, and nations, particularly in a world presently plagued by intercultural distrust, religious and racial hatred, and the pervasive problems of terrorism and violence. To realize a soberly empowered humanity of peace and progress, Dr. Sheikh Nizamudhin Sulthan is dedicated himself to the ethical transformation of human subjectivities across the diverse spectrum of world cultures. He strives realize this by keenly guiding the global center of peace, Jeelani Shareef; leading the pious and humanitarian mission of Nizamia educational initiatives; organizing scholarly leadership under the Ghausiyya Ulama Council; commanding the platform for Sufi propagation and social welfare through the Jeelani Study Centre and its moral-cultural publications; and providing humanitarian healthcare and philanthropic support to the needy through the powerful platform of Jeelani Public Charitable Trust (Reg. No. 0569/04/2006).
