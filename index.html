Who We Are

Jeelani Trust is a public charitable organization dedicated to foster positive change where piety meets humanity, leading pious, productive, and progressive initiatives in Kerala-India and outside. We champion Sufi piety, educational advancement, and social welfare, serving as a vital platform for moral guidance and impactful initiatives in health, humanitarian aid, and socio-religious reform. Functioning as an inclusive public venture, Jeelani Trust actively promotes Sufi path of excellence, fosters educational progress, advocates for social welfare, and offers moral guidance to empower society as a whole.

More than just an organization, Jeelani Trust is a comprehensive public platform, illuminating the path through the propagation of Sufi piety, the unwavering promotion of education and social well-being, and the dedicated practice of moral guidance and human development for the global humanity. With a profound vision of ethics and welfare, the services of Jeelani Trust reach out to the needy people in society at large, beyond the distinctions of community, culture or caste. The key objectives of the Trust are to promote world peace, moral progress and communal harmony.

Taking forward the selfless and soulful mission laid out by the astute founder, Quthbuzzaman Dr. Shaikh Yousuf Sulthan Shah Qadiri Chishti, the current chairman and successor, Naeb Quthbuzzaman Dr. Shaikh Nizamudhin Sulthan Shah Qadiri Chishti, meticulously ensures significant progress, mobilizing wider public to the virtuous path of welfare and progress, realized through Jeelani Shareef and developed under Jeelani Charitable Trust (reg. no.Â 0569/04/2006).

Our Vision

Ethically mending and progressively transforming the current human condition, the practical moral guidance and dedicated social services spearheaded by Jeelani Trust are meticulously designed to actualize the profound vision of humanity's unity, ushering in an era of world peace, justice, and harmony.

The Trust envisions a world where every individual lives with moral integrity, social dignity, wholistic health, and access to knowledge, empowered to achieve their full spiritual and human potential.

Championing the Sufi path of moral empowerment, guided by the teachings of Holy Prophet Muhammad (peace and blessings be upon him), strengthened by the spirit of Imam Ali (k.w.) and upheld by the great Sufi Shaikh â€˜Muhyiddinâ€™ Abdul Qadir al-Jeelaniâ€™s spiritual order, Jeelani Public Charitable Trust is actively striving to realize a just and peaceful global society where compassion, understanding, and equitable opportunities universally flourish.

Our Mission

Under the steadfast leadership of its Chairman, His Holiness Naeb Quthbuzzaman Dr. Sheikh Nizamudhin Sulthan Shah Qadiri Chishti, Jeelani Trust carries forward the foundational objectives set by the revered Sufi Guru, His Holiness Quthbuzzaman Dr. Sheikh Yousuf Sulthan Shah Qadiri Chishti. Continuing the profound legacy of its founder, the current leadership firmly advances the noble mission of Jeelani Trust, expanding its vital work of promoting Sufi perfection of human life, sponsoring developments in education and health, helping the poor and needy, and spreading peace and harmony in society. Based at Jeelani Shareef in Kerala, the Trust's core commitment remains to serve society and nurturing a morally robust humanity.

Upholding our wider vision and addressing the pressing needs of the time, Jeelani Trust keeps devising effective plans and projects, and systematically carries them out on regular basis. The Trust conducts programmes and runs institutions for promoting holistic well-being, expanding educational development, healthcare initiatives, and humanitarian aid, grounded in the principles of Sufi piety and socio-religious reformation, for the benefit of the deserving and under-served communities within the region and around the world.
