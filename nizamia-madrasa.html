Nizamia Madrasa

A thriving international educational initiative, Nizamia Madrasa is an online Islamic leaning platform, offering foundational religious schooling and moral education for the children, run by Jeelani Trust, since December 2021. Under the pious patronage of the Holy Sufi Guru, Holy Naeb Quthbuzzaman Dr. Sheikh Nizamudhin Sulthan Shah Qadiri Chishti, Nizamia Madrasa stands as a fast-flourishing venture, catering to a wider international community of students with foundational teaching in Quran, Prophetic tradition, Arabic language, Islamic history and Sufi moral lessons.

Within Jeelani Shareef lies the holy Maqam (dargah) of Sufi Hazrat Quthbuzzaman, a sacred spiritual space where Structured as a seven-year, with daily classes at Primary, Secondary, and Higher Secondary Levels, currently, the Madrasa functions at 6 levels, including the pre 1 – preparatory and two open-general (weekend) batches. With a primary focus on the students from the state of Kerala, and extended to the national and international enrollment, including students from other states in India and from Arab Gulf countries, the current enrolment of the students ranges up to 400. Nizamia Madrasa, following the moral and social mission founded by the Sufi Shaikh Hazrat Quthbuzzaman and enthusiastically carried forward by his successor, Hazrat Shaikh Naeb Quthbuzzaman, was established in December 2021, with a wider provision the finest foundation for children's Islamic learning – online - fully free of cost. As a comprehensive online learning programme, Nizamia Madrasa ensures the education of younger generation in moral tradition of Islam through the most advanced technological support.
