Jeelani Public Charity Services

With a profound vision of human development, social empowerment, and humanitarian welfare, Jeelani Public Charitable Trust is dedicated to reach out to the needy people in society, beyond the distinctions of community, religion or caste. Taking ahead the pious humanitarian mission intimated by the devout founder of the Trust, revered Sufi Guru Hazrat Quthbuzzaman, the Jeelani movement for social and spiritual change vigourously widened the horizon of hope for social and human development aided by the pious philanthropic support and social service, under the Sufi successor and the current Chairman, Holy Guru Hazrat Naeb Quthbuzzaman Dr. Sheikh Nizamudhin Sulthan Shah Qadiri Chishti.

Through all its organizational organs connected to Jeelani Shareef, the devoted mission of Jeelani Trust, spearheaded by Dr. Sheikh Nizamudhin Sulthan and centred at Jeelani Shareef, aims to ensure soulful service to humanity.

Stands as spiritual edifice of global significance, Quthbuzzaman Jeelani Shareef strives to foster peace and The numerous social welfare activities and public charity initiatives undertaken by Jeelani Trust significantly operate at the intersection of educational empowerment, health & medical care, environmental protection, career counselling to youth, supports to public safety and civic amenities, etc.

The unique centre of healthcare and public wellness, Thaqdees Hospitals Ltd. - Cochin -Kerala, established by the founder of Jeelani Trust in 2002, and revitalized by the robust developments initiated by the current Chairman since 2019, stands as a fast-growing venture of healthcare and social service, fostering wholistic wellbeing and human development – the cherished values of Jeelani Trust.

The futuristic vision and progressive mission behind the thriving educational enterprise of Nizamia Institute and Nizamia Madrasa represent the longstanding tradition of the Trust, piously providing free education while ensuring cutting-edge quality and contemporary relevance. The continuing educational aid for renovation and frequent provision of infrastructural supports to various public and private schools, colleges and excellence centres also prompt concerned people from all socio-religious backgrounds to reach out to the benevolent mission centred in Jeelani Shareef.
